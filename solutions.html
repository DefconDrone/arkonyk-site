Three solutions, one platform

Descriptors.com, FeeSuite, and ARKITEKT each solve a distinct payment challenge. Buy them independently or together for visibility, optimization, and strategy across the full payment lifecycle.

D

Descriptors.com

A payment descriptor management platform. The descriptor is the merchant name a cardholder sees on their statement — and one of the most overlooked causes of disputes. Visa estimates 50%+ of cardholder disputes begin because a customer doesn't recognize a transaction.

  • Register, manage & validate descriptor portfolios
  • Associate logos & business info; identify conflicts
  • Improve recognition for merchants, acquirers & issuers

FeeSuite

A payment cost management and reconciliation platform. Fees are scattered across processors, banks, networks, and thousands of line items — so overcharges go undetected. FeeSuite turns processing costs into clear, actionable intelligence.

  • Interchange, assessment & markup analysis; effective rate
  • Deposit reconciliation & statement validation
  • Executive dashboards, benchmarking & fee forecasting
