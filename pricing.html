FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — the Starter plan includes a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

Can I use consulting without the software?

Absolutely. Our advisory and implementation services are available standalone, though software customers receive preferred rates.

How does transaction-based pricing work?

Each plan includes a monthly transaction allowance. If you exceed it, you'll only pay a small per-transaction overage — and we'll flag when an upgrade saves you money.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans come with two months free versus monthly billing.
