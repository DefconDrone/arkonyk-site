About Arkonyk

Truth in Payments.

Arkonyk is a payments intelligence company — solving longstanding problems in new ways for the banks, merchants, and customers who move money every day.

Our mission

Arkonyk — our name merges two guiding principles: to lead, and to aspire. Our customers are Acquiring Banks, Issuing Banks, and the Merchants and Cardholders they serve. We are focused on solving longstanding problems in new ways, leveraging our experience, relationships, and innovative technology.

Our story

Clarity across the payment lifecycle.

Payment descriptors, processing fees, and acceptance strategy are among the most overlooked — and most expensive — parts of the payments ecosystem. Confusing statements drive disputes; fragmented fees hide overcharges; complex stacks grow without a blueprint.

Arkonyk brings these together. Descriptors.com reduces confusion and disputes, FeeSuite reveals and reduces cost, and ARKITEKT — our advisory practice — designs the strategy. Each solves a real problem on its own; together they cover the complete payment lifecycle.
