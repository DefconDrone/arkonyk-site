About Arkonyk

We make money movement boringly reliable.

Arkonyk was founded on a simple belief: businesses deserve financial software that's accurate by design — and partners who actually understand payments.

Our story

Born from the frustration of broken payment stacks.

After years inside finance and payments teams, our founders kept seeing the same thing: powerful processors bolted onto spreadsheets, reconciliation done by hand, and no one who could connect the technical and the financial.

So we built Arkonyk — software that treats money with the precision it deserves, backed by consultants who've personally run the migrations, negotiations, and audits our customers face. Software and expertise, from one team.
